Alcohol-free passionfruit martini


Just as good as the alcohol equivilant, this mocktail is a real crowd pleaser.

Prep: 5 mins
Serves: 2

Ingrediants

  • 3 passion fruits, halved
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 egg white
  • 100ml alcohol-free spirit
  • 2 tsp sugar syrup
  • handful of ice
  • sparkling grape juice, to serve

Method

Step one

Scoop the flesh from four of the passion fruit halves into a cocktail shaker.
Add the lemon juice, egg white, spirit and syrup and shake vigorously until frothy.

Step two

Scoop the flesh from four of the passion fruit halves into a cocktail shaker.
Add the lemon juice, egg white, spirit and syrup and shake vigorously until frothy.

Step three

Top up the martinis with the grape juice and garnish with the remaining passion fruit halves.
